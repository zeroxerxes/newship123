Reliable transport solutions

Leave your transport of consumer goods and industrial cargo safely with Blue Water. With almost half a decade of experience, we provide a wide range of services completing your logistics chain

Customised Freight forwarding

Whether you need a domestic or international road logistics solution for your full truck load, part load, groupage or temperature-controlled products, we can arrange and deliver your cargo when and where you wish.

Our skilled and experienced teams of Freight forwarders arrange deliveries from door to door of all kinds of palletised industrial cargo or consumer goods throughout Europe and in the rest of the world.

We have a large fleet of modern trucks. However, we are more than a traditional transport company handling cargo from one point to another.

Our individual solutions focus on our clients' needs, and we aim to be the best local and global Freight forwarder – our clients' preferred transport partner.

Sustainable road transport

Everyone has a responsibility in the green transition, and it does not have to be difficult.

With a mobile and flexible pallet rack from SpaceInvader, Blue Water can create value all the way through your logistics chain. The rack makes it possible to double-stack cargo before, during and after the transport. In this way, the innovative solution lets you achieve:

  • Savings on CO2 emission
  • More cargo in each truck
  • Cost savings
  • Optimised supply chain
  • Pallets in ergonomic work height

The advantages are many. The result wins your company savings on CO2 emissions, time and optimises utilisation of excess air. All of it important on the sustainable journey.

We make road transports run smoothly

Key Account Management

Our Key Account Management team analyses your transport flow and explore ways of optimising your transport solution. With wide-ranging market knowledge, we enjoy working closely together with our customers.

Whatever transport need you may have, this solution offers you an assigned Blue Water contact and 24/7 assistance whenever needed. 

Sustainability

Green transports are the future, and we have entered partnerships to cooperate in supporting sustainability when moving our customers’ cargo.

Using the transport system powered by SpaceInvader, we can improve the capacity of trucks and thereby reduce environmental impact and costs. That is what we call a win-win situation. 

Solutions Service

At Blue Water we are specialised in moving cargo exceeding standard dimensions. If your cargo needs special attention, our Solutions Service team can provide solutions customised to your requirements.

Your transport will be conducted in compliance with safety and efficiency no matter what project transport - oversized or out of gauge cargo - you need moved. 

Innovative IT solutions

Transparency is important to us. That is why we offer innovative IT solutions making it possible for our customers to easily book and follow transports.

Our own booking system MyBWS is built with focus on giving our customers a smooth experience when booking with Blue Water. If your logistics chain demands it, we also offer integrable IT solutions.

When choosing MyBWS you get the opportunity to:

  • Book across transport modes
  • Calculate delivery time
  • Print labels directly from the system
  • Set up templates easing your next bookings
  • Notify end-user

Exhibitions and trade fairs

As members of the International Exhibition Logistics Association (IELA) we follow certain standards for quality, safety, customer focus and precision. We handle transports to more than 1000 exhibitions and trade fairs each year for customers of various sizes.

Blue Water offers transport to and from location, documentation and customs clearance, delivery to stand, unpacking and erection of stand, packing and storage. 

Blue Water is part of our DNA

The greatest asset of our company is the people creating our solutions. We have a diverse staff, but one thing is for sure; all Blue Water employees work together to create the best possible solutions for our customers.

We believe in keeping our promises and always thinking outside the box to provide innovative and efficient transport and logistics solutions.

We do it all

What type of transport fits my business?

Depending on your needs, we will find the right solution for you. With insight into your business, we can set up a solution fitting and improving your supply chain. Our solutions cover every transport mode to and from every corner of the world.

Customs solutions

One of the Blue Water core competences is our in-house team of customs specialists. We have a unique focus on solving customs issues and easing the customs clearance procedures by being proactive. With a global setup and local know-how, we are always up to date.

Cargo insurance

Protect your cargo by taking out a cargo insurance at a favourable premium. Our insurance offers better coverage than the average cargo insurance. We can also advise you on Incoterms.

Groupage

We collect and deliver groupage shipments and distribute across Europe via our own terminals and a European network of terminals.

Part Loads

If you need to ship more than six pallets, why not take advantage of our part load solution. We collect your cargo and deliver directly.

Full Loads

Full load trailer transport is a fast and reliable service. As we use GPRS trailers, you will always know where we are.
