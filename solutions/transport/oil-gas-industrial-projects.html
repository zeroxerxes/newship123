Worldwide project transports

Transport by road, sea and air to the Oil & Energy sector as well tailored solutions for oversize and heavy project cargo to companies within all types of industries

Oil rigs and out-of-gauge cargo

Blue Water Shipping is a global provider of transport and logistics solutions to Oil, Gas and Industrial Projects. Through our extensive network of offices at major offshore locations around the globe, we provide our clients with a large variety of services ranging from small courier shipments to project transports of complete oil rigs from one continent to another.

Our core competences include challenging projects to the Caspian region, e.g. transport of entire oil rigs through the Russian Volga Don river system and transport and logistics for FPSO projects worldwide.

Our project teams - consisting of experienced specialist Freight forwarders, port captains, naval architects, stevedore foremen and HSSEQ experts - offer the highest level of personal service and transport and logistics solutions with HSSEQ as a top priority.

Our teams deliver precise planning for all types of projects combining local expertise with global knowledge.

Projects

Logistics Services

Out-of-gauge

Whether you need road transport for a large reel in Europe or you need a tug boat to arrive in Angola as soon as possible – we can arrange.

Areas of expertise

Engineering

Our dedicated and skilled in-house engineers solve the technical elements for a large variety of complex transport assignments. They deliver top quality because your cargo is valuable for them as well.

Caspian

With strong representation in the Caspian region, we have solved challenging transports since the nineties. We have built valuable knowledge of local conditions. That is why we call ourselves local experts.

HSSEQ

We provide the proper training and information and encourage a ‘zero incident' mindset to protect our most important asset – our employees. We work according to international standards within safety.

Route planning and studies

We are experienced in route and feasibility studies. Providing thoroughly detailed transportation plans and method statements ensures a clear description of the operational work and safety measures that we take into consideration for your project. We create a transport plan, based on our route surveys, port surveys, loading and unloading studies, risk assessments, and take local and global legislation, permits and documentation into consideration.

Our attention to details is a prerequisite for a job well done.

Project transports experts

Global Network

We cooperate with a large variety of carefully selected business partners worldwide – all based on important criteria such as quality, high service and reliability.

Project Management

For more than 40 years, we have been involved in a large variety of projects around the globe. We have provided full Project Management to tailor-made solutions.

Supercargoes

Our supercargoes ensure that the stevedores provide safe and effective unloading and loading. Our specialists travel around the world to supervise all stages of the projects.

Track record for challenging projects

We have the in-house expertise to transport your challenging project cargo and delicate items for the Oil and Gas industry. Despite the complexity of large projects, we will always be able to create a solution, specifically designed to fulfil your needs, by using our strong global network of specialised employees and partners.

For us, flexibility is the keyword for large projects. By being flexible, we can manage every step of your project in a safe manner from the beginning to the end – as show in our track record.
