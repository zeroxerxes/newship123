Your experienced port agent

With local knowledge, we offer 24/7 one-point-of-contact port agency services to all types of vessels

Local expertise and service to crew and shipowners

The Blue Water Port Service teams work with local knowledge and a global understanding offering comprehensive agency and stevedoring solutions. Through an exten­sive network our flexible and agile organisation makes us capable of meeting whatever need you may experience.

Since 1972, Blue Water has offered a wide range of international shipping and Freight forwarding services. This puts us in a favourable position to custom­ise services and let our clients focus on their core task while we provide valuable supporting activities.

As a top player in wind logistics, we act as port agents and stevedores in connection with offshore wind projects including port set-up, handling and transport of wind components. 

Our outlined standards for HSSEQ applies worldwide, and we are certified according to ISO9001, ISO14001 and ISO45001. We are members of BIMCO, the Danish Shipbrokers and Port Operators and we hold a FONASBA certificate.

Wide-ranging services within port agency

Remote Attendance

As Remote Attendance agents we can coordinate the port call from a distance. This provides a flexible solution at a reduced cost.

Husbandry

Through innovative husbandry solutions, we offer our Port Services directly to vessel owners, operators and technical management.

Passage service

Near the Danish straits, we offer a wide range of passage related services such as launch boat deliveries, tank cleaning and disposal of washing water.

Bunkering

Different types of fuel for different types of vessels – we provide it all. Whether delivered by barge, truck or ex pipe, we offer competitive rates.

Rental services

Our rental services cover fenders, gangways, containers, baskets, generators, waste skips, portable office and welfare units and terminal setup.

Repair works

Vessel repair involves a lot of practical jobs and logistics challenges. We have the in-house expertise to provide all the services needed for a smooth operation.

Crew change

From a shipowner’s point of view, Denmark has very favorable immigration rules, enabling us to offer smooth crew change operations at low rates.

Offshore support and chartering

At Blue Water we have been actively involved in offshore logistics, base management, project transport, chartering, stevedoring and all-round support since 1972.

Yard stays

We provide tailor-made services at all Danish shipyards, and through our local knowledge you get the benefits of professional, reliable and time efficient solutions.

Decommissioning

Decommissioning of rigs, platforms and wind turbines can be a challenging task. With decades of experience, it is an everyday job to our experienced teams.

Working safely anywhere

Safety is paramount to Blue Water. Through a zero incident-mindset we take active measures to ensure a healthy and safe working environment for all em­ployees, clients, business partners and subcontractors.

The standards for HSSEQ are outlined in our Code of Conduct.

Our HSSEQ Department facilitates global initiatives for improvements in our daily work, which you can read about here.

Your local customs specialist

Avoid delays of your shipments in the customs clearance process. Our in-house customs experts can handle all procedures and customs documentation for you. You will benefit from our worldwide network and local specialists having extensive knowledge of local rules and regulations.

We are also fully updated on the Brexit situation, which you can read more about here.

Secure and customised warehousing solutions

Do you need space to store your pallets, production machinery, industrial cargo, wind turbines, perishables or any other types of goods? We offer secure indoor and outdoor warehousing facilities, whether you need short or long-term storage.

We can also assist with pick & pack, labelling, registration, packing / repacking and much more. Please contact us and let us find the best warehousing solution for you.
