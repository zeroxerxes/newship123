North Atlantic transport specialists

With a strong representation in the North Atlantic region, Blue Water offers transport by Road, Sea and Air

We make logistics in the North Atlantic easy

Since the eighties, Blue Water has operated in the North Atlantic countries. We are represented all over the North Atlantic region with own offices, and we work in a close cooperation with local agents. This makes it possible to reach any North Atlantic destination.

Holding a strong position as supplier of transport and logistics to the North Atlantic, we provide all kinds of transports solutions - from door-to-door deliveries to large projects and complete Supply Chain Management.

To us, it is not only about getting from A to B. We make sure to find possibilities for optimisation and savings in your logistics chain. Our experience with the – at times – challenging infrastructure makes us experts in handling the most difficult transports.

Supplements to the main transport, such as customs formalities and cargo insurance are of course also part of our services. With local presence we provide favourable rates, and our customers are always in focus when we create solutions.

Local presence in the North Atlantic countries

Greenland

For 30 years, we have been present in Greenland, and today our set-up includes five local offices. 

Iceland

From the capital of Iceland, Reykjavik, we offer transport solutions to, from and within the country.

The Faroes

Through our well-established network of partners, we can also handle your transport to the Faroes.

Departures by sea and air

Cold storage and high-quality reefer transport

Frozen and chilled seafood require good protection and the correct temperature during storage and transport to maintain the good North Atlantic quality. We monitor and control that the quality of your product remains of high standard all through the transport.

We operate own reefer terminal approved for storage of fresh, chilled and frozen products. By means of modern technology, we handle, sort and provide door-to-door deliveries of refrigerated products.

Our close cooperation with producers from the North Atlantic, Norway and the Baltic States ensures that fresh products are delivered to consumers in the rest of Europe.
