Your logistics expert in Finland

Blue Water is a global provider of all logistics services in modern supply chain management. At the heart of Blue Water is an organisation of professionals skilled to offer complete and tailor-made solutions to any shipping and transport requirement anywhere in the world.

  This page is also available in Finnish  

Safe supplies since 1972

Our goal is to provide our customers with the best service and the highest quality in the industry. We already serve 60 offices in the global main market areas. At each Blue Water office, we combine our strong global network and know-how with local expertise.

In this way, we ensure safe and efficient Freight forwarding for our customers. Blue Water Finland's professional employees are an active part of our international network. We serve Finnish export and import companies in all modes of transport, in all directions.

Customised logistics solutions

We also offer our customers an easy booking system, MyBWS. Our booking system allows you to process transport orders for your shipments even faster and more efficiently.

MyBWS is an innovative booking system based on the needs of our customers, which combines all stages of the supply chain into one work process. The system requires no installation and is easy to set up, so you can start processing your orders more efficiently without delay.

In MyBWS, you can create a tracking metric and order templates that meet your needs, making it easy and quick to place your next shipping order. So focus on your core tasks in peace and let us handle the shipments for you!

Three good reasons to choose MyBWS

1. Save time with an efficient ordering system

2. Get professionals to handle your shipments

3. Easy to set up, no installation required
