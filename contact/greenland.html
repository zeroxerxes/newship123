Your logistics expert in Greenland

With many local offices Blue Water Greenland is your One-Stop-Shop when you need cargo moved from A to B

Local knowledge through more than 30 years

We have own offices in Nuuk, Qaqortoq, Sisimiut, Aasiaat and Ilulissat and a strong agent network making it possible to cover the entire North Atlantic.

From Greenland we ship thousands of tonnes of fish products to Denmark to further distribution on the European Continent and overseas as well as consumer goods from Europe to the North Atlantic markets. Additional services such as customs clearance, COD and cargo insurance are also a part of our service package.

With our strong position as suppliers of transport and logistics solutions to Greenland, we offer all kinds of package solutions and door-to-door delivery.

We offer full Supply Chain Management and create solutions to all parts of the supply chain – easily adapted to the products and needs of the customers. With roots in the local community and local employees, it comes naturally to us.

Freight solutions

Domestic

We help private customers as well as companies with moving all sizes and types of cargo between all cities by sea, road and air.

Sea Freight

Sea Freight is one of our core activities. We deliver standard consumer goods and temperature-controlled products to and from Greenland.

Air Freight

With daily departures between Scandinavia and Greenland, we are experts in handling air Freight import and export shipments.

Local expertise

Our expert knowledge ensures a smooth course. You are guaranteed an accurate customs clearance each time you import or export.

Marine Logistics

Blue Water is experts within transport and logistics solutions to the Cruise and Marine industry. We offer storage, supplies and distribution of all necessities to vessels and cruise ships calling at port in Greenland. In Greenland, we further offer direct-to-ship containers.

A long-standing and close cooperation with some of the world’s largest cruise lines has granted us with strong knowledge of this fast-growing industry.

We are port agents for

Cruise ships

We offer port agency to cruise ships in all Greenlandic ports. We have been doing this for many years, and along with our comprehensive services within Marine logistics, it makes us a leading player within the Cruise and Marine industry.

Trawlers

We have great experience with trawlers to which we offer a wide range of services such as unloading, packing and clearing of containers, delivery of launch boats, diving surveys, tank cleaning and disposal of waste water.

Other vessels

Our experienced Port Agency team offers 24/7 One-Point-of-Contact services and supplies to all types of vessels. For example, we can deliver water and electricity, line handling and our office can inform of weather and ice conditions.

Moving services

We do moving jobs for private customers and companies, and we handle the entire job from beginning to end. When moving to or from Greenland we can make sure that the correct documentation is in place.

Our well-educated and local employees have many years of experience with handling moving jobs in Greenland.

On all our offices you can buy equipment to the moving: packing boxes, bubble wrap, wrapping paper and mattress bags.

Please contact us to get a non-binding quote for your moving job.

Warehouse solutions

Surveillance

All our storage facilities are equipped with modern surveillance which is your guarantee for safe storage of your goods day and night.

Rent a container

We let containers from our warehouses in all of Greenland and can often deliver from day to day. We can also store your container. 

Rent a storage room

If you need safe storage of your goods or an extra storage room for your company, it is safe and easy to rent a storage room from us.

Digital stock management

All goods are stored digitally. The goods are marked with labels and scanned when entering the warehouse and once again when leaving.

Pick & Pack

As company you can speed up the process in our new Pick & Pack warehouse. We make sure that your orders are picked, packed and delivered to you and your customers.

WMS

With our Warehouse Mangement System you can easily check stock, which goods are up for delivery and follow each delivery all the way to the customer.

Cleaning

In Qaqortoq and Aasiaat we offer cleaning services to private customers and companies. We perform all kinds of cleaning tasks – from daily cleaning to window cleaning.

Our local service assistants are the best at what they do, and they are selected after personality, professional competences and experience.
