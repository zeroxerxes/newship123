Our local experts in Rodby

The office in Rodby was established to facilitate provision of services to the Fehmarn Belt project, for which we deliver port services to the ships and project clients. Further, the office covers Lolland and the adjoining island of Falster with loading and discharging of vessels, general agency services, warehousing and transport & logistics
